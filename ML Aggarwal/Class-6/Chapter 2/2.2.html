Question 1. Determine the sum by suitable rearrangement:

(i) 432 + 368 + 219
(ii) 1983 + 647 + 217 + 353

Answer:

(i) 432 + 368 + 219
= (432 + 368) + 219
= 800 + 219 = 1019

(ii) 1983 + 647 + 217 + 353
= (1983 + 217) + (647 + 353)
= 2200 + 1000 = 3200

Question 2. Determine the product by suitable rearrangement:

(i) 2 × 1658 × 50
(ii) 4 × 239 × 25
(iii) 625 × 20 × 8 × 50
(iv) 16 × 125 × 625 × 7

Answer:

(i) 2 × 1658 × 50
= (2 × 50) × 1658
= 100 × 1658 = 165800

(ii) 4 × 239 × 25
= (4 × 25) × 239
= 100 × 239 = 23900

(iii) 625 × 20 × 8 × 50
= (20 × 50) × (625 × 8)
= 1000 × 5000 = 5000000

(iv) 16 × 125 × 625 × 7
= (16 × 625) × (125 × 7)
= 10000 × 875 = 8750000

Question 3. Find the value of the following by using suitable properties:

(i) 493 × 8 + 493 × 2
(ii) 24576 × 99 + 24576
(iii) 15625 × 159 – 15625 × 59
(iv) 5281 × 92 + 5281 × 8

Answer:

(i) 493 × 8 + 493 × 2
= 493 × (8 + 2) (by distributive property)
= 493 × 10 = 4930

(ii) 24576 × 99 + 24576
= 24576 × 99 + 24576 × 1 (by distributive property)
= 24576 × (99 + 1)
= 24576 × 100 = 2457600

(iii) 15625 × 159 – 15625 × 59
= 15625 × (159 – 59) (by distributive property)
= 15625 × 100 = 1562500

(iv) 5281 × 92 + 5281 × 8
= 5281 × (92 + 8) (by distributive property)
= 5281 × 100 = 528100

Question 4. Find the product using distributive property:

(i) 740 × 105
(ii) 245 × 1008
(iii) 189 × 99
(iv) 203 × 98

Answer:

(i) 740 × 105
= 740 × (100 + 5) (by distributive property)
= 740 × 100 + 740 × 5
= 74000 + 3700 = 77700

(ii) 245 × 1008
= 245 × (1000 + 8) (by distributive property)
= 245 × 1000 + 245 × 8
= 245000 + 1960 = 246960

(iii) 189 × 99
= 189 × (100 – 1) (by distributive property)
= 189 × 100 – 189 × 1
= 18900 – 189 = 18711

(iv) 203 × 98
= 203 × (100 – 2) (by distributive property)
= 203 × 100 – 203 × 2
= 20300 – 406 = 19894
